About

Here we summarise the baseline characteristics of individuals hospitalised with COVID-19 and individuals previously hospitalised with influenza. A preprint of this research is has been released on medRxiv, with the study protocol available here.

This is a network cohort study that has been developed by the Observational Health Data Sciences and Informatics (OHDSI) collaborative. All analytic code used in the study is available at GitHub. Further collaboration is invited, and the results presented here will be updated as more data becomes available.
