Study status

Describes the current study status. The following states are defined:

Repo CreatedThe study repository has just been created. Work has not yet commenced.
StartedA first commit was made (to something else than the README file). Work has commenced.
Design FinalizedThe protocol and study code have been finalized.
Results AvailableThe study results are publicly available, for example in a paper or results explorer app.
CompleteThe study is complete, no further dissemination planned.
SuspendedThe study has been suspended, and may or may not be continued at a later point in time.
