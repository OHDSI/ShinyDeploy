Description

The performance of the model including the operating characteristics at different risk cutoffs, the overal discrimination and the overal calibration.

Tabs

The three tabs are:

  • The 'Summary' tab shows the prediction question being explores and various operating characteristics for a range of risk cutoffs (the threshol bar is itneractive and enables you to explore different values by moving the bar left or right)
  • The 'Discrimination' tab shows the AUROC, AUPRC, predicted risk distributions and F1 score
  • The 'Calibration' tab shows the generic calibration plot and the calibration per age group and gender.
